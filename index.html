Welcome

This project was created as an educational tool to learn about different types of cells and microorganisms.

Bacteria

Types of Bacteria: Cocci, Bacilli, Spirilla, Vibrio, Spirochetes

Advantages: Nitrogen fixation, digestion aid, fermentation

Disadvantages: Can cause diseases, food spoilage

Virus

Types of Viruses: DNA viruses, RNA viruses, Retroviruses

Advantages: Used in genetic engineering and vaccines

Disadvantages: Cause infections like HIV, COVID-19

Plant Cell

Organelles:

  • Cell Wall: Provides structure
  • Chloroplast: Site of photosynthesis
  • Vacuole: Stores nutrients and waste

Animal Cell

Organelles:

  • Nucleus: Controls cell functions
  • Mitochondria: Produces energy (ATP)
  • Lysosome: Digests worn-out organelles
